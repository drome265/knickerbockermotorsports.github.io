Car 19

The 2013 car improved on the designs of its many predecessors, most notably it was the first car to have an aero kit. Unfortunately, the aero kit did not get used at competition and the car also ran out of gas during endurance. However, the fact that all hardware held up shows the vehicle was robust and well tested prior to competition.
