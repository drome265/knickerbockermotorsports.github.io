2017-2018 Team

KMR-18

With the desire to push for greater innovation, we transitioned to a two year cycle of production. This required a year off of competition, but brings us with potential to create a better product. We now have two cars being produced in parallel at two different phases of development. This car will include a dry sump system designed in-house among other firsts for the team.
